WHO IS SIMON CAPON?

Simon Capon is a Trainer and master practitioner of a psychology called Neuro-linguistic programming. (NLP). Over the last twenty years he has brought together a collection of psychological skills and techniques to produce a program that is dedicated to the development of sports individuals and teams.

No matter if you’re a keen amateur or a world class competitor in the making one of your biggest challenge you will face is the battle in your head. Often, we are told by supposed experts that only a selected few have the ability to remain focused whilst under extreme pressure. The reality is that everyone is in possession of everything required to perform under extreme pressure with clarity, courage, and certainty. This is possible by adopting some remarkably simple yet highly effective skills through a face-to-face meeting, video conference call or the revolutionary training incognito.
